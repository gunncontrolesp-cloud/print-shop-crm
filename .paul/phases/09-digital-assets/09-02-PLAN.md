<objective>
## Goal
Add an "Assets" tab to the customer portal where customers can view and download all artwork files that were marked as customer assets by the shop.

## Output
- `/portal/assets` page listing all customer asset files with download links
- "Assets" link in portal navigation
- Files sorted by most recent, grouped by job if more than one order
</objective>

<acceptance_criteria>

## AC-1: Customer can see their assets in portal
```gherkin
Given a customer is logged into the portal
  and the shop has marked at least one of their files as an asset
When they click "Assets" in portal navigation
Then they see a list of their asset files with name, date, and download link
```

## AC-2: Only customer_asset files shown
```gherkin
Given a customer has files where is_customer_asset = false
When they view the portal assets page
Then only files with is_customer_asset = true are shown
```

## AC-3: Empty state when no assets
```gherkin
Given no files are marked as assets for this customer
When they view the portal assets page
Then they see "No artwork files yet. Your files will appear here once your job is complete."
```

</acceptance_criteria>
