<objective>
## Goal
Generate a QR code for each job that staff can scan with their phone to update the job's production stage without going to a desk or navigating the full dashboard.

## Purpose
Production floor workers interrupt their work to walk to a desk or hunt for the job on a tablet. A phone-scannable QR code on each job ticket eliminates that friction and keeps the production board current in real time.

## Output
- QR code displayed on job detail page (admin can print or show on screen)
- QR encodes URL: `[APP_URL]/scan/[jobId]`
- `/scan/[jobId]` route: staff-authenticated, shows job name + current stage + single "Advance Stage" button
- Scan page optimized for phone: large text, large button, auto-logout after action (optional)
- Stage advance from scan page uses same `advanceJobStage` action as production board
</objective>

<acceptance_criteria>

## AC-1: QR code on job detail
```gherkin
Given an admin/manager views a job detail page
Then a QR code is displayed that encodes the scan URL for that job
  and a "Print" or "Copy Link" button is available alongside it
```

## AC-2: Scan page accessible to staff
```gherkin
Given a staff member scans the QR code with their phone
When they are logged in to the app
Then they see: job name, customer name, current stage, Advance Stage button
```

## AC-3: Stage advance works from scan page
```gherkin
Given a staff member is on the scan page
When they tap "Advance Stage"
Then the job stage advances to the next stage
  and the page shows "Updated to [new stage]" confirmation
  and the production board reflects the new stage (Supabase Realtime)
```

## AC-4: Login redirect for unauthenticated staff
```gherkin
Given a staff member scans the QR but isn't logged in
When the scan URL loads
Then they are redirected to /login with a return URL
  and after login they land back on the scan page
```

</acceptance_criteria>
